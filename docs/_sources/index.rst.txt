Welcome to hoclights's documentation!
=====================================

.. image:: _static/img/hoclightsLogo.png
   :scale: 50 %
   :alt: alternate text
   :align: center

Hoclights is a small "toy-project" developed at `i3lab <http://i3lab.polimi.it>`_ in Politecnico di Milano. It is an experiment to play with the integration of web technologies, the underlying middlewares and physical smart objects.
